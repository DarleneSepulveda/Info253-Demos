Bad Poetry

By: Darlene Sepulveda

The following are effortless poems, with grammatical errors:

Midnight, is the time where black cats roam, where luck is lost and forests are not homes.
Midnight, is a form of quiet, as people go and rest, sleep is their own makeshift diet.
Midnight, is when people lie awake, restless and sweaty, their bed is too warm, their mind isn't steady.
Midnight, is students, who lie awake and study, turning assignments in late, when they know it isn't ready.

Unsteady eyes and pounding heads, Warm pillows and comfy beds.
Intense typing and sore backs, Fighting the urge to have a snack.
Aching shoulders and numb feet, the whirring fan from the heat.
Suffering from eye strain, computer work can be a drain.
